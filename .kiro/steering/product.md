# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
Exam Fever Simulator is an AI-powered mock exam generator that creates high-pressure, timed practice tests from uploaded student PDFs. It helps students prepare for real exams by simulating the stress and time constraints they'll face, while generating relevant questions from their own study materials.

## Target Users
**Primary Users**: Students preparing for exams
- Need realistic exam practice under time pressure
- Want to test their knowledge of specific study materials
- Require personalized practice tests based on their own PDFs
- Benefit from AI-generated questions that match their content

## Key Features
- **PDF Upload & Analysis**: Upload study materials and textbooks for content extraction
- **AI Question Generation**: Gemini 1.5 Flash analyzes PDFs to create relevant exam questions
- **Timed Mock Exams**: High-pressure, time-constrained practice sessions
- **Multiple Question Types**: Various formats (multiple choice, short answer, essay prompts)
- **Performance Analytics**: Track progress and identify weak areas
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and personal progress tracking
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices

## Business Objectives
- **Student Success**: Help students improve exam performance through realistic practice
- **User Engagement**: Create an engaging, stress-testing environment that builds confidence
- **Content Personalization**: Leverage students' own materials for maximum relevance
- **Scalability**: Support multiple users with efficient PDF processing and question generation

## User Journey
1. **Sign Up/Login**: Create account or authenticate via Supabase
2. **Upload Materials**: Upload PDF study materials (textbooks, notes, handouts)
3. **AI Processing**: Gemini 1.5 Flash analyzes content and generates question bank
4. **Configure Exam**: Set time limits, question count, and difficulty level
5. **Take Mock Exam**: Complete timed practice test under simulated pressure
6. **Review Results**: Analyze performance, see correct answers, identify improvement areas
7. **Iterate**: Upload more materials or retake exams to improve

## Success Criteria
- **User Engagement**: Students regularly use the platform for exam preparation
- **Performance Improvement**: Users show measurable improvement in practice scores
- **Content Quality**: AI-generated questions are relevant and appropriately challenging
- **Technical Performance**: Fast PDF processing and smooth exam experience
- **User Satisfaction**: Positive feedback on exam simulation realism and usefulness
